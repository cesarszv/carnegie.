The idea

A calmer place for relationship context.

Keep notes clear

Separate enduring details from recent context, so relationship notes stay useful instead of becoming a messy document.

Remember the moments

Log meaningful conversations, meetings, calls, and moments in a simple timeline you can return to when it matters.

Find the thread

Return to people, notes, and moments from one place, without relying on memory or spreadsheets.

See connections quietly

Understand how people relate to each other without turning your relationships into a public social platform.

Principles

Built for thoughtful recall.

  • Private access only; Carnegie is not open to the public.
  • Owned by you, not shaped around a social feed.
  • Quiet enough for personal life, structured enough for professional life.
